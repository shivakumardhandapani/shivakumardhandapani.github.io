About

I'm an Electrical and Computer Engineer with an M.S. from Northeastern University, specialized in Hardware and Software for Machine Intelligence. My work sits at the crossroads of robotics perception, computer vision, and applied AI.

Over the past few years, I've built LiDAR-IMU SLAM systems, 3D reconstruction pipelines, autonomous vehicle perception models, and predictive analytics systems — always focused on translating research into real-world impact.

Before grad school, I worked as a Systems Engineer at Infosys, building .NET/Angular enterprise applications and honing my full-stack development skills. I'm driven by the challenge of making autonomous systems smarter, faster, and more reliable.
